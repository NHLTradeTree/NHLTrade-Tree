Trade History

Hampus Lindholm

Acquired through a trade with the Anaheim Ducks.

  • 2022 Trade: Bruins received Hampus Lindholm and a 2022 2nd-round pick from Anaheim. The Ducks received Urho Vaakanainen, John Moore, and Bruins' 2022 1st-round pick, plus 2023 and 2024 2nd-round picks.
  • John Moore: Signed by the Bruins as a free agent in 2016.
  • Urho Vaakanainen: Drafted by the Bruins in 2017 (18th overall).
  • 2022 1st-round pick: Original Bruins pick traded to Anaheim.

Morgan Geekie

Acquired via trade with the Seattle Kraken.

  • 2024 Trade: Bruins traded their 2024 3rd-round pick to Seattle for Morgan Geekie.
